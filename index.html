Project Overview & Implementation

The Hand of Hope project is developed through four strategic stages.

🚀

Stage 1

Project Initialization: research user needs, technology feasibility, and smart cane concept design.

🛠️

Stage 2

MVP Development & Testing: hardware integration, AI features, and real-world user testing.

💰

Stage 3

Commercialization with 30M VND funding, production scaling and community outreach.

🌏

Stage 4

Future Expansion into a Social Enterprise with mobile apps, AI upgrades and regional growth.
